# 🚀 StudyQuest

A fun, free study app for kids. **Study. Quiz. Play. Level up!** 🧠⚡

Pick your grade, choose a subject, and learn your way — read or write study
guides, take a graded mock quiz, or play a fast arcade game. Works in any web
browser with no install and no sign-in.

## How to use

1. Open **`index.html`** in any browser (or visit the live site).
2. Tap your **grade** (1–8 or Higher).
3. Pick a **subject**: Math, Science, Geography, Shapes, Words, or Fun Facts.
4. Choose what to do:
   - **📝 Mock Quiz** — practice and get a grade (A–F) with a review of misses.
   - **🎮 Play Game** — a fast 60-second challenge with streaks and high scores.
   - **📖 Study Guide** — write your own notes (and add a photo of them).
   - **✏️ My Questions** — make your own questions to use in the quiz & game.

## Features

- 🎚️ **Four difficulty levels** per subject — Easy, Medium, Intermediate, Hard.
- 🎓 **Grades 1–8 + Higher** — math problems scale to your grade.
- 📝 **Mock quizzes** with letter grades and a "review your mistakes" list.
- 🎮 **Arcade game mode** with combos, a per-question timer, and high scores.
- 📖 **Study guides** you write yourself — attach a photo of your notes too.
- ✏️ **Custom questions** — build your own; they show up in the quiz and game.
- ⏱️ **Study timer** — focus sessions (5–45 min) with an alarm when time's up.
- 🔊 Sound effects with a mute button. Everything saves in your browser.

Everything is one self-contained HTML file — no accounts, no internet needed,
nothing to install. 100% free.
