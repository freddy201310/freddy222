# 🚀 StudyQuest

A free study app for **children and young adults** — learn anything and level up
your brain. 🧠⚡ Works in any web browser, no install and no sign-in.

## What you can do

- **🎯 Subjects** — Math, Science, Geography, Shapes, Words, Fun Facts. Each has:
  - **📝 Mock Quiz** (graded A–F with a review of misses)
  - **🎮 Play Game** (fast 60-second arcade with streaks & high scores)
  - **📖 Study Guide** (write notes, attach a photo of them)
  - **✏️ My Questions** (make your own — they appear in the quiz & game)
- **🪄 Game Builder** — paste or type *any* lesson / study guide and the app
  instantly turns it into a playable quiz **and** game. Put one fact per line:
  ```
  France - Paris
  Photosynthesis = how plants make food
  What is 6 x 7? : 42
  ```
  You can also attach a photo of your study guide for reference.
- **⭐ Levels & XP** — earn XP from quizzes, games, and study time, and level up.
- **⏱️ Study Timer** — focus sessions (5–45 min) with an alarm; earns XP.
- **👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Zone** (PIN-protected) — parents can:
  - Track progress: level, quizzes, average score, games, study time, day streak.
  - Set **reward goals** like *"Reach Level 5 → $10"* or *"Score 90% → movie night."*
    These show up on the child's home screen as motivation.

> 💡 Rewards are just a promise a parent makes to their child. **StudyQuest never
> handles any money or payments** — there is no billing of any kind.

## Notes

- Everything is one self-contained HTML file. No accounts, no internet needed,
  nothing to install — 100% free.
- All progress, guides, custom questions, and built games are saved in your
  browser on your device.
- The Parent PIN is a simple family lock stored on the device (not a bank-style
  login).
