# 🚀 Quiz Blitz!

A fast-paced, fun study game for kids (ages 8–11). Answer as many questions as
you can in 60 seconds, build streaks for combo bonuses, level up as you go, and
chase a new high score!

## How to play

1. Open **`index.html`** in any web browser (just double-click it — no install needed).
2. Pick a topic: **Math**, **Science**, **Geography**, **Words**, **Fun Facts**,
   or **🎲 Surprise Me!** for a mix of everything.
3. Tap the right answer before the timer bar runs out.
4. Answer correctly in a row to build your **streak** — bigger streaks = more points!
5. Every 4 correct answers bumps you to a harder **level**.
6. When the 60 seconds are up, see your score, best streak, and accuracy. Beat your
   high score!

## Features

- 🎲 Five topics plus a mixed "Surprise Me" mode
- ➕ Math questions are generated endlessly and get harder as you level up
- 🔥 Streak combos with a score multiplier (up to x5)
- ⏱️ Per-question countdown bar — answer fast for bonus points
- 🔊 Built-in sound effects (toggle with the speaker button)
- 🏆 High score saved in your browser
- 📱 Works on phones, tablets, and computers — no internet required

It's a single, self-contained HTML file with no dependencies. Safe to copy
anywhere and play offline.
