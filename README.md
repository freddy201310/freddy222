# Lumina

**Learn smarter.** A free study platform for students — master any subject,
build your own study games, and track your progress. Runs in any web browser
with no install and no sign-in.

## Features

- **Subjects** — Math, Science, Geography, Shapes, Words, Fun Facts. Each offers:
  - **Mock Quiz** (graded A–F with a review of misses)
  - **Play Game** (fast 60-second arcade with streaks & high scores)
  - **Study Guide** (write notes, attach a photo of them)
  - **My Questions** (write your own — they appear in the quiz & game)
- **Game Builder** — paste or type *any* lesson / study guide and Lumina turns it
  into a playable quiz **and** game instantly. One fact per line:
  ```
  France - Paris
  Photosynthesis = how plants make food
  What is 6 x 7? : 42
  ```
- **Levels & XP** — earn XP from quizzes, games, and study time, and level up.
- **Study Timer** — focus sessions (5–45 min) with an alarm; earns XP.
- **Parent Zone** (PIN-protected) — track progress (level, quizzes, average score,
  games, study time, day streak) and set **reward goals** like
  *"Reach Level 5 → $10"* that show on the student's home screen as motivation.

> Reward goals are just a promise a parent makes — **Lumina never handles any
> money or payments.**

## Notes

- One self-contained HTML file. No accounts, no internet needed, 100% free.
- Progress, guides, custom questions, and built games are saved in your browser.
- The Parent PIN is a simple on-device family lock, not a bank-style login.
